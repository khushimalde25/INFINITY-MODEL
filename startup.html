Attero Recycling (India)
Domain:

E-waste & Li-ion battery recycling

Key Information:
  • One of India's largest e-waste recycling companies.
  • Founded by Nitin & Rohan Gupta.
  • Recycles metals like copper, gold, lithium, cobalt.
  • Operates a major recycling facility in Roorkee.
  • Uses patented technology for high-purity metal recovery.
  • Partners with OEMs for EV battery recycling.
LOHUM Cleantech (India)
Domain

Battery recycling & second-life repurposing

Key Information:
  • Specializes in Li-ion battery recycling.
  • Uses NEETM® process for low-carbon recycling.
  • Processes ~10,000 tonnes of batteries per year.
  • Provides EPR services for OEMs.
  • Repurposes batteries before recycling to reduce waste.
  • Expanding operations in US & Europe.
